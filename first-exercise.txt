This is a story about Flomar. Flomar is a Flomar; she is unclassifiable as anything else than a Flomar. She a hungry little Shanghai beastie who feeds on cuddles, foodz, and intimidation. She's invincible.
Flomar came from a land far far away to live in the glory of California. On this journey, she's met many journeymates, but her main homie is Dr. Clarious Meow.
Flomar and Dr. Clarious Meow pretend they're not best friends, but they are. They may not cuddle, but their secret language is evidence of their silent agreement of home domination and territories.
